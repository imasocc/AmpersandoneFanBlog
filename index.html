Who Are AMPERS&ONE?

AMPERS&ONE are a 7-member international boy group under FNC Entertainment. The group features Kamden (USA), Brian (Canada), Jiho (Korea), Siyun (Korea), Kyrell (USA), Mackiah (Australia), and Seungmo (Korea). They officially debuted on November 14, 2023 and have quickly become loved for their bright energy, addictive songs, and close bond with their fandom, ANDEAR.

Since debut, AMPERS&ONE have released five projects: AMPERSAND ONE, One Hearted, One Question, Wild & Free, and Loud & Proud. In spring 2025, they held their first U.S. tour and later joined P1Harmony as openers, bringing their music to even more international fans.

Quick Facts

  • Group name: AMPERS&ONE (엠퍼센드원)
  • Company: FNC Entertainment
  • Members: 7 (Kamden, Brian, Jiho, Siyun, Kyrell, Mackiah, Seungmo)
  • Debut date: November 14, 2023
  • Fandom name: ANDEAR
  • Releases: AMPERSAND ONE, One Hearted, One Question, Wild & Free, Loud & Proud
  • Recent activities: First U.S. tour (Spring 2025) & opening for P1Harmony (Fall 2025)
